Flow (2-Step Confirmation):
1. Frontend creates Express Checkout Element (no PaymentIntent yet)
2. Customer interacts with Apple Pay / Google Pay
3. Frontend calls stripe.createConfirmationToken()
4. Backend receives token, creates PaymentIntent with confirm: true
5. Backend creates a separate Transfer to connected account
6. Frontend shows result
